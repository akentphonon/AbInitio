Optional Exercises

Time permitting, try the following additional exercises:

  1. Rerun the unconstrained dynamics, only this time use 10 electronic minimization iterations between every ionic MD timestep (instead of 5). Plot the constant of motion and compare the magnitudes of the fluctuations and drift with those you obtained in your original run.
  2. Continue the unconstrained methane dynamics simulation for another 400 timesteps. Append the coordinates for the continuation to the original xyz file (you may want to copy over the original first so you have both). Look at the velocity autocorrelation function for the twice-longer simulation. Have the peak widths changed? What effect does longer simulation time have on the resolution of the power spectrum?
  3. Try rerunning the unconstrained dynamics simulation with larger initial displacements. Analyze the velocity autocorrelation function and power spectrum. What is the effect on the peak amplitudes?
